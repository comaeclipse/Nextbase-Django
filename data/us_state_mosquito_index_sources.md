# US State Mosquito Index — sources

`us_state_mosquito_index.csv` — per-state mosquito pressure index (0–100), rank
(1 = worst), and qualitative band. Used by the `/critters` page.

## Sources used to calculate the index

- NOAA National Centers for Environmental Information (NCEI)
- Current Results Weather and Science Facts
- U.S. Geological Survey (USGS)
- Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)
- Climate Central

## Additional mosquito-data resources (discussed, not used for every state score)

- VectorSurv
- National Ecological Observatory Network (NEON)
- CDC ArboNET
