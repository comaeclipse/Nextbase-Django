# Raytheon USA Job Locations Sources

Retrieved: 2026-07-10

## Scope

This seed captures official RTX/Raytheon careers locations that are presented as Raytheon work or hiring locations in the United States. It is not a full RTX enterprise location list and does not include Collins Aerospace or Pratt & Whitney locations unless the page explicitly identifies the location as Raytheon.

The RTX Workday feed was checked for live posting data, but exhaustive posting-detail aggregation hit rate limiting. The imported rows therefore use stable official RTX careers location pages and the Raytheon site-tour page as source-of-record inputs. `ActivePostingCount` is intentionally blank until a throttling-safe Workday snapshot importer is added.
