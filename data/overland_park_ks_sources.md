# Overland Park, KS Source Notes

Retrieval date: 2026-07-07.

## Geography

- Primary geography: Overland Park city limits, Johnson County, KS.
- Population and density: city limits. Density is calculated from Census QuickFacts July 1, 2025 population estimate, 203,677, divided by 2020 land area, 75.18 square miles, yielding about 2,709 people per square mile.
- Housing: Zillow city-level ZHVI for Overland Park, KS, data through 2026-05-31.
- Cost of living: ERI city cost-of-living estimate says Overland Park is 7 percent less expensive than the national average; stored as index 93.
- Sales tax: combined Overland Park rate from the city budget/tax page and Kansas Department of Revenue July 2026 rate publication listing.
- Income tax: Kansas top individual income-tax rate, 5.58 percent.
- VA access: Overland Park VA Clinic, 10500 Mastin Street. Distance is approximate driving distance from Overland Park City Hall to the clinic.
- Elections: Johnson County official final results for 2024; 2016 Johnson County returns were cross-checked against Johnson County's official 2016 PDF and the county table on the 2016 Kansas presidential-election page. Percent fields are two-party winner share, rounded to whole percent. Trend fields use two-party share: Republican share fell from about 51.4 percent in 2016 to about 45.7 percent in 2024, so `rep_vote_share_change_pp = -5.8` and `dem_vote_share_change_pp = 5.8`.
- Crime: `TCI` is a violent-crime-rate proxy indexed to the 2024 FBI national violent-crime rate. Kansas KBI reports Overland Park PD 2024 violent crime rate of 2.0 per 1,000 residents, or about 200 per 100,000. Using FBI's 2024 national rate of 359.1 per 100,000 gives about 56; using the report's raw violent offense count, 385, over population 196,875 gives 195.6 per 100,000 and an index near 54. The stored TCI is 54.
- Weather: annual rain, snow, sun days, January low, and July high use BestPlaces Overland Park climate summaries; July humidity uses Time and Date's Overland Park climate table.
- LGBTQ score: HRC 2025 Municipal Equality Index final score, 91. State policy score uses MAP Kansas overall score, -1/49.
- Economic hubs: `tech_hub=Y` because Overland Park hosts major engineering/technology and fintech employers, including Black & Veatch's headquarters and the Fiserv Aspiria campus project. `defense_hub=N` because the city has no major active-duty installation or comparable defense cluster.
