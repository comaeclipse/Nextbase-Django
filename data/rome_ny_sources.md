## Geography

- Primary geography: Rome city limits, Oneida County, NY.
- Population and density: city limits. Density is calculated from Census QuickFacts July 1, 2025 population estimate, 31,682, divided by 2020 land area, 74.85 square miles, yielding about 423 people per square mile.
- Housing: Zillow city-level ZHVI for Rome, NY, data through 2026-05-31.
- Cost of living: ERI city cost-of-living estimate says Rome is 5 percent less expensive than the national average; stored as index 95.
- Sales tax: combined Rome / Oneida County rate of 8.75 percent.
- Income tax: New York top individual income-tax rate, 10.90 percent.
- VA access: Donald J. Mitchell Rome VA Clinic, 125 Brookley Road. Distance is approximate driving distance from Rome City Hall to the clinic, rounded for display.
- Elections: Rome city ward/district presidential returns from Oneida County detailed exports. 2016 city totals: Clinton 4,230 and Trump 6,733 after combining New York fusion-party lines. 2024 city totals: Harris 5,063 and Trump 7,550 after combining Democratic/Working Families and Republican/Conservative lines. Percent fields are two-party winner share, rounded to whole percent. Trend fields use two-party share: Republican share fell from about 61.4 percent in 2016 to about 59.9 percent in 2024, so `rep_vote_share_change_pp = -1.6` and `dem_vote_share_change_pp = 1.6`.
- Crime: `TCI` is a violent-crime-rate proxy indexed to the 2024 FBI national violent-crime rate. OpenCrime reports Rome's 2024 violent crime rate as 237.5 per 100,000, based on FBI city data. FBI's 2024 national rate is 359.1 per 100,000, so 237.5 / 359.1 * 100 = 66.1, rounded to 66. OpenCrime's safety grade is B.
- Weather: annual rain, snow, and sun days use BestPlaces Rome climate summaries; January low and July high use WeatherSpark; July humidity uses Time and Date's Rome climate table.
- LGBTQ score: no Rome-specific HRC Municipal Equality Index score was located. State policy score uses MAP New York overall score, 44.5/49, converted to 91 on a 0-100 display scale for `lgbtq_rating`.
- Economic hubs: `tech_hub=Y` and `defense_hub=Y` because Rome hosts the AFRL Information Directorate/Information & Spectrum Warfare work, Griffiss Institute, and the Eastern Air Defense Sector at Griffiss Business and Technology Park.
